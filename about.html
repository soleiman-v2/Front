Soleiman Arish was born in 1989 in Balochistan, Iran. He studied Architectural Engineering at the International University of Chabahar from 2009 to 2013 and completed his Master's degree in Architecture and Creative Practice for the City and Landscape at the University of Bologna from 2021 to 2024. Soleiman has collaborated with various architects and engineers in Iran and established his own firm, SAA Office, from 2018 to 2021. He is currently working at Integrated Design Srl in Bologna (2023-present).
